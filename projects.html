Clicker Game (Swift)

A simple tap-to-feed mini game: tap to feed the sheep at the center, watch the counter go up, and beware—overfeeding makes the sheep explode and ends the game. Core loop is working; more content and mechanics are planned.

Meowstronaut (Swift)

A space cat pilot dodging traps and collecting points. Implemented: Home, Shop, and Leaderboard screens. Planned: swipe control to navigate, hit-to-end rule, top-10 leaderboard, and spending points in the shop for power-ups. Currently in active development.

GitHub Repo

Zappy (PM Case Study)

An immersive smart-facility concept designed to transform ordinary trash bins into interactive, engaging, and efficient public-space companions. Built through product research and strategy rather than coding, the project explores user pain points, market opportunities, and sustainable design. Implemented: user interviews, blue-ocean positioning, core feature definitions, and a B2B2C revenue model. Planned: pilot testing, hardware integration, and a full financial roadmap to evaluate ROI and scale across theme parks, schools, and commercial venues.
